About Us

Great Men in History was originally formed in January of 2008 on Destromath and rerolled on Proudmoore in September of 2015. Many of our members started playing in Vanilla, and we're a "serious raiding guild"-turned-"friends and family guild" (with the love of min-maxing firmly intact). We're a mature group of players looking for friendly (and competent) team members.

Raiding

Fridays, 6pm - 9pm PST

Recruitment

We're looking for members who want to join a smaller guild (we want to keep the raid group at around 15-20) with the expectation that you care about your class/role and come prepared.

We're ideally looking for members who are mature, friendly, and flexible. We want people who won't mind subbing in to DPS if we have too many healers or subbing in to tank if someone can't make a raid night.
