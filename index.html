Recruitment

Looking for a Heroic* raiding guild? Great Men in History is looking for players who want to raid in Legion!

* We may switch to Mythic raiding in Legion.

We're not actively recruiting, but we're always happy to talk to exceptional DPS (especially those willing to off-spec tank or heals)!

Expectations

  • Show up on time and ready to go.
  • Maintain a high rate of attendance. We understand that life happens, but you must be willing to reach out and let us know of conflicts ahead of time if at all possible.
  • Study up on strategies before the raid.
  • Install and use Ventrilo.
  • Install and use the following addons:
    • RCLootCouncil
    • DBM
    • Future encounter-specific addons as needed (e.g., Iskar Assist)
  • RSVP to raids on the calendar.

If this sounds like a good fit for you, fill out our quick application to get started.

About Us

Great Men in History was originally formed in January of 2008 on Destromath and rerolled on Proudmoore in September of 2015. Many of our members started playing in Vanilla, and we're a "serious raiding guild"-turned-"casual raiding guild" (with the love of min-maxing firmly intact). We're a mature group of players looking for friendly (and competent) team members to fill out our roster for Legion.
