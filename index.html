About Us

Great Men in History was originally formed in January of 2008 on Destromath and rerolled on Proudmoore in September of 2015. Many of our members started playing in Vanilla, and we're a "serious raiding guild"-turned-"casual raiding guild" (with the love of min-maxing firmly intact). We're a mature group of players looking for friendly (and competent) team members to fill out our roster.

Raiding

Fridays, 6pm - 9pm PST + Scheduled Mythic Dungeons

Progression: 7/13 Heroic HFC

Recruitment

We currently have a consistent raid group of ~15. We're looking for another solid main tank and we're willing to consider solid DPS of any kind. Definitely fill out an app if you're interested in joining us!

Current Needs

  • 1 Tank

*We're willing to trial quality DPS, so apply if you're interested in joining us.

Expectations

  • Show up on time and ready to go.
  • Maintain a high rate of attendance. We understand that life happens, but you must be willing to reach out and let us know of conflicts ahead of time if at all possible.
  • Study up on strategies before the raid.
  • Install and use Ventrilo.
  • RSVP to raids on the calendar.

If this sounds like a good fit for you, fill out our quick application to get started.
